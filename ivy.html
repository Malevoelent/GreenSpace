  • Enrich the soil with compost and fertilizer before planting.

Placement:

  • When removing the container of the plant, make sure not to damage the roots.
  • Position the ivy in the hole.
  • Fill the hole with the amended soil, gently firming it down to remove air pockets.

After the planting process:

  • Water the newly planted ivy thoroughly to settle the soil around the roots.
  • Lastly, apply a layer of organic mulch around the base of the plant, keeping it away from the stem to prevent rot.
