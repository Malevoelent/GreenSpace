Weeping Fig

Preparation:

  • Pick the place where it gives light only in the morning as the plant canâ€™t handle harsh afternoon brightness.
  • Use a well-draining potting mix, like a blend of regular potting soil with perlite or sand to improve drainage.
  • Make sure the hole is a twice big as the root.

Placement:

  • Gently remove the weeping fig from its nursery pot.
  • Place it in your chosen location or pot.
  • Then fill the hole back with soil mix.

After the planting process:

  • Water the soil until the bottom drains it then let it dry before watering it again.
  • Apply a balanced liquid fertilizer every two weeks during the spring and summer months.
  • Make sure you mist the leaves regularly or place the pot on a pebble tray of water as they prefer high humidity.
