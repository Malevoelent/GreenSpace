Peace Lily

Preparation:

  • Select a pot with drainage holes, and ensure it's large enough for the plant's size.
  • Use a well-draining potting mix, preferably one designed for indoor plants.

Placement:

  • If the plant will be moving pots, gently lift the plant up and place it in its picked pot.
  • Choose a spot where the peace lily gets bright, indirect light, because the direct sunlight can scorch the leaves.

After the planting process:

  • Keep the soil consistently moist, but not soggy, after finishing the previous process.
  • Allow the top inch or two of the soil to dry out before watering again another time.
  • Peace lilies thrive in humidity so make sure to mist the leaves regularly or place the pot in a tray of pebbles filled with water.
