ZZ Plant

Preparation:

  • Provide bright, indirect light, as ZZ plants tolerate low light.
  • Use a well-draining potting mix with perlite or sand for better drainage.

Placement:

  • When switching pots, make sure the hole is a twice as big as the root.
  • Fill back the pot with soil.

After the planting process:

  • Water the plants for a few times after planting.
  • Check when the soil dries out after watering each time to repeat the process again.
  • Make sure it stays in an average room temperature.
  • Fertilize monthly during spring and summer using a diluted all-purpose houseplant fertilizer.
  • Propagate by leaf or stem cuttings, and repot every spring as needed.
